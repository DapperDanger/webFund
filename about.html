ABOUT US

OUR GOAL

We here at MINIMAL strive to grant our customers a better selection of fashion wear at an affortable price without sacrificing its comfort and elegance. With the help of over thousands of creative designers and employees from all over the world, we here at MINIMAL provide a large variety of fashion style to fit you for every occasion.


FOUNDER


 It all started with the founder of MINIMAL, Mr. Yohji Yamamoto - an extremely talented designer with a big visionary in the line of fashion whom had open the first MINIMAL fashion store in the city of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia back in 1972. Mr. Yamamoto is also involved in many parts of iconic fashion designs thoughout history.
