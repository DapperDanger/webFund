SPRING/SUMMER 2018 | MILAN FASHION WEEK


Milan Fashion Week (Italian: Settimana della moda) is a clothing trade show held
semi-annually in Milan, Italy. The autumn/winter event is held in February/March of each year,
and the spring/summer event is held in September/October of each year.

VICTORIA'S SECRET FASHION SHOW 2015


    The Victoria's Secret Fashion Show is an annual show sponsored by and featuring Victoria's
Secret, a brand of lingerie and sleepwear. Victoria's Secret uses the show to promote and
market its goods in high-profile settings. The show features some of the world's leading
  fashion.The show is a lavish event with elaborate costumed lingerie, varying music by    
   leading entertainers, and set design according to the different themes running within the show.
